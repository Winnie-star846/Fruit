Persimmon

Core functions: clearing heat and promoting fluid, moistening lung and relieving cough, detoxifying.
Sex taste: cool, sweet taste, astringent
Suitable people: lung heat Yin deficiency caused by cough, dry throat dry mouth, mouth sores, blood in the stool, hemoptysis
Not suitable people: spleen and stomach deficiency cold people
Note: Tannic acid in persimmon has astringent effect, which can cause constipation by consolidating stool.

Persimmon taste sweet astringent, cool, into the heart, lung, large intestine meridian. Persimmon cool and clear heat, sweet cool and produce fluid to quench thirst, can be used for fever tiredness and thirst. Persimmon gan cool Shengjin can moisten lung dryness, for lung heat cough.

Persimmon contains a small amount of carbohydrates, vitamins, trace elements, including iodine, vitamin C and carotene content is relatively rich.
