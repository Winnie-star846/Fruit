Winter jujube generally in the Gregorian calendar October to November natural maturity, because the northern weather into the cold earlier, so the name "winter jujube". Winter jujube taste sweet, smooth, can benefit the heart, moisten the lung, spleen, stomach, nourishing blood to calm the mind, regulating 100 drugs, antidote poison, with the effect of reducing blood pressure, vitamin supplement.

1. Lower blood pressure
Winter jujube is rich in vitamin C and vitamin P, which is beneficial for healthy capillaries and maintaining the elasticity of blood vessel walls, especially vitamin P in winter jujube, which helps to reduce blood pressure.
2. Take vitamin supplements
The vitamin C content of winter jujube is high, which is called "living vitamin pill", and eating more winter jujube helps to supplement the vitamin.

nutritive value
Winter jujube is rich in sugar, protein, vitamin A, vitamin C, vitamin E and potassium, sodium, iron, copper and other trace elements.
